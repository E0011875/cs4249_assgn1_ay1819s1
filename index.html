Welcome!

In this experiment, you will be working with two types of menus – Marking and Radial using two kinds of pointing devices – trackpad and mouse. Your task is to select the correct menu item as indicated in each trial as accurate and as quickly as possible.

The experiment is split into two parts – part A and part B with a 3-minute break in-between. Each section comprises of 36 trials and should take less than 15 min to complete. You will be prompted to take a break once you have completed the first section.

Ensure that you have filled up a consent form prior to the experiment to allow data collected from this experiment to be used for research purposes. Also, ensure that you are given sufficient practice with both menus before proceeding with the main experiment.

At the end of the experiment, we would like you to fill up a post-questionnaire to hear about your experience and how it can be further improved.

A participant number ID should have been passed to you. This number will help us track your progress throughout the experiment to collect the relevant experimental data.
